PROJECTS

PhotoQuest

PhotoQuest is an Android app that a few classmates and I made to solve the problem of reverse image searching. Imagine being able to take a picture of any object, press a button, and receive information about that item. It's like Googling but for pictures.

AI and Embedded Systems iRobot

I was the team lead on the project and managed the tasks of each memember as well as made sure we met all of the requirements. We used C to manipulate IR and Sonar data to create a map of the surroundings and navigate our robot around an obstacle course.

Our design implements the MIPS instruction set. We used VHDL to virtualize the machine and run test benchmarks. This was a semester long project that caused many sleepless nights and large consumptions of caffeine. The design worked perfectly and we ended up getting 100% on the whole thing!
